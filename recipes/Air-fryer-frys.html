Air Fryer Fries

These crispy fries are easy to make.
They are delicious and quick to prepare.
Cheap too.

Ingredients

  • Frozen fries
  • An air fryer

Steps

  1. Take fries out of freezer
  2. Pre-heat air fryer
  3. Take fries out of bag
  4. Put in air fryer tray
  5. Cook for ten minutes(shake occasionally)
  6. Serve
