Smash-Burgers

Description

The smash burger is a simple classic.
Very easy to make with the right tools and incredibly delicious cheap too.

Ingredients

  • 18% fat Ground beef
  • brioche burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Measure beef into 85 gram chunks
  2. Roll beef into balls
  3. Get your cast iron pan screaming hot
  4. Place your beef ball on the grill and smash it with your burger smasher
  5. Flip the burger with your scraper
  6. Add salt and place cheese on top
  7. Remove form grill when the cheese has melted
  8. Toast the buns
  9. Serve and enjoy
