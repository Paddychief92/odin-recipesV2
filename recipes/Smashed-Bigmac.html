Smashed Big Mac

Description

The Smashed Big Mac burger is a simple classic.
Very easy to make with the right tools and incredibly delicious cheap too.

Ingredients

  • 18% fat Ground beef
  • brioche burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Salt
  • Big mac sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Measure beef into 85 gram chunks
  2. Roll beef into balls
  3. Chop the onions and pickles
  4. Get your cast iron pan screaming hot
  5. Place your beef ball on the grill and smash it with your burger smasher
  6. Flip the burger with your scraper
  7. Add salt and place cheese on top
  8. Remove form grill when the cheese has melted
  9. Toast the buns
  10. Add sauce to the buns
  11. Add the lettuce onions onions and pickles
  12. Serve and enjoy
